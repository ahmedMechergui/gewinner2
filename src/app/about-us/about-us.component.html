Change Your Life

GEWINNER was founded on 2017 and was officially lunched on 2019 by four cofounders. Our startup makes a point of honor to carry out all its missions in the strict respect of certain values which represent the pillars of its activity: Innovation, Performance, Rigor and Customer Orientation.

  • GEWINNER offers innovative and customized solutions in the healthcare industry. The challenge we face is simple “Change Your Life!”: continue to anticipate and meet the needs of disabled people and their entourage, in order to offer them the best services and the most competitive offers.
  • Our first product is MOOVOBRAIN, a steering system and remote control of an electric wheelchair for people who can’t use their upper members to pilot with the joystick. It is based on Brain/Machine interface and artificial intelligence. MOOVOBRAIN is a combination of several disciplines voice assistance, artificial intelligence and IOT.
  • We offer also wheelchair’s accessories that can facilitate the piloting and ensure user’s safety. Some other before and after sales services can be requested.
